About

Who are we?

We are a group of individuals who miss the times of a healthy discussion. We host events in multiple locations and build our community. We want to urge others to take a step back, and enjoy the moment they are in. Set aside your social media for the night and join us for a night of fun!

What's it like?

The events includes a three course meal catered by out partner Rooted Delights. They focus on vegan delicious eats. After and during your meal, you will hear from different typed of entertertainment- smooth soul singers; instrumental, and spoken word. When you arrive at the event we urge people to lock up their phone for hours or so and unplug. We have our host lock phones in a secure location. Locking your phone up for the night, enters you in a raffle and a chance to win some prizes! After your three course meal, we encourage people to stay an have some real talk discussions with no judgements. We are all apart of a community your voice is important. We will have topics, but feel to pick your own topic of discussion and debate.

We also discuss how we can better ourselves and our community; the more the merrier!
